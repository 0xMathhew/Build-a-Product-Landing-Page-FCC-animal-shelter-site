About Us

Our Story

In the winter of 2018, a severe cold snap hit our region, leaving local municipal facilities overwhelmed. A small group of neighborhood volunteers rallied together, opening up their own garages and barns to provide emergency shelter for a dozen stray and abandoned dogs. What was meant to be a temporary rescue mission quickly exposed a much larger, permanent need in our community. By the spring of 2019, those volunteers officially founded Dog Shelter.

What began as a grassroots rescue network has since grown into a fully equipped, modern sanctuary dedicated to the rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming of dogs in need. Over the past several years, we have expanded our facilities to include dedicated medical recovery spaces, enrichment play yards, and an active behavioral training program designed to help every dog put their best paw forward.

Our Mission

We believe that every dog, regardless of their past, age, or breed, deserves a safe haven and a dedicated advocate. Our work goes far beyond simply providing food and shelter; we are committed to holistic rehabilitation.

Every dog that enters our gates receives:

  • Comprehensive veterinary care and nutritional support.
  • Tailored behavioral assessments and socialization.
  • An active matchmaking process to ensure they find the perfect, permanent match for their unique personality and energy level.

We operate on the firm belief that a shelter shouldn't just be a temporary holding block, but a transformative bridge to a vibrant new life. Whether you are looking to adopt your first companion, volunteer your time, or support our medical fund, you are helping us write the next chapter of our story—and theirs.
